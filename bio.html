My Life

The man of the hour

My name is Letlotlo Matlali, I am a mosotho male aged 24. I am currently pursuing a degree in software engineering at Limkokwing University Lesotho.

To completely understand me, you need to know where I come from. I am the son of one Makalo Matlali and 'Malemohang Matlali, who live at Ha-'Masana Maseru.
They have married for over 30 years and have three children.
I am their second born and their only son.
